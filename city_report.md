**Tokyo Travel Guide**

**Weather in December 2025**

December brings festive illuminations, holiday events, and a special year-end atmosphere with cooler temperatures. Weather: Cold winter days (5°C-12°C). Generally sunny but chilly.

**Top Things to Do in Tokyo in December 2025**

1. Explore the Tokyo Ice Sculpture Festival.
2. Enjoy winter illuminations.
3. Experience cultural events and holiday festivities.
4. Visit popular attractions like the Tokyo Tower, Meiji Shrine, and Imperial Palace.

**Accommodation**

* **Hotels**: Book your hotel room in advance to ensure availability. Consider staying at a hotel near major attractions like the Tokyo Tower or Meiji Shrine.
* **Vacation Rentals**: Rent an apartment or house for a more authentic experience. Look for places with heating facilities, as temperatures can drop significantly.

**Food and Drink**

* **Local Cuisine**: Try traditional Japanese dishes like sushi, ramen, and tempura.
* **Winter Foods**: Warm up with hot chocolate, coffee, or soup.

**Tips and Reminders**

* Dress warmly, especially in winter.
* Bring a portable charger for your devices, as cold weather can drain batteries quickly.
* Check the weather forecast before heading out to avoid any unexpected rain showers.

**Festivals and Events**

* **Tokyo Ice Sculpture Festival**: A must-visit event that showcases intricately carved ice art.
* **Winter Illuminations**: Experience Tokyo's festive atmosphere with beautifully lit streets and buildings.
* **Year-End Parties**: Celebrate the end of 2025 at one of Tokyo's many year-end parties.

**Getting Around**

* **Subway**: Use the efficient subway system to navigate Tokyo easily.
* **Buses**: Take a bus to explore the city's outer districts.
* **Taxis**: Hail a taxi for a convenient ride, but be prepared for traffic congestion.

**Safety and Precautions**

* Be mindful of cold weather conditions, such as frostbite or hypothermia.
* Always carry a portable charger and keep your devices charged.
* Respect local customs and traditions, especially during festivals and events.
