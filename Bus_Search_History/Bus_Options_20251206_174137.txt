## 3. Practical Tips for Buses

- **Booking advice:** Book in advance, especially during peak seasons (holidays, weekends) to secure your seat and potentially get better prices. Use platforms like redBus, MakeMyTrip, AbhiBus, or Zingbus to compare prices and bus options.
- **What to expect:** Bus travel can be affected by traffic, especially during peak hours. Confirm the boarding point and arrival point beforehand. For overnight journeys, consider buses with sleeper options for better comfort. Check for amenities like charging points, Wi-Fi, and entertainment options when booking for a more comfortable journey.
