## 3. Practical Tips for Flights

*   **Booking advice:**
    *   Book at least 1-2 months in advance for better prices. The best time to book is roughly 1 month prior to the flight date, and the worst time is about 13 weeks prior.
    *   Compare prices on multiple websites like Skyscanner, ixigo, and the airline's official website.
    *   Be flexible with your travel dates if possible, as prices can fluctuate significantly.
    *   Consider booking connecting flights, as they are often cheaper than direct flights.
*   **What to expect:**
    *   Flights from Toronto to Hyderabad typically involve one or two layovers.
    *   Ensure you have the necessary travel documents (passport, visa, etc.).
    *   Pack essential items in your carry-on luggage in case of delays.
    *   Check baggage allowance policies for each airline.
    *   Confirm terminal and gate information before heading to the airport.
    *   Prices are heavily influenced by demand and seasonality; expect variations.
    *   Consider travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances such as flight cancellations or medical emergencies.
